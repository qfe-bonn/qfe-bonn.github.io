About Us

Meet the team behind the QFE Bonn and learn about our mission.

Mission

What We Stand For

We are dedicated to bridging the gap between academic theory and industry practice in the field of quantitative finance and economics. We aim to provide students with the knowledge, skills, and connections needed to succeed in this rapidly evolving field.

We value diversity of thought, collaborative learning, and practical application. Through workshops, competitions, research projects, and industry connections, we strive to create a community where students can explore their interests and develop the skills needed for successful careers in the field.

Community

Who We Are

Quantitative Finance & Economics Bonn (QFE Bonn) is a student-led learning and research organization at the Economics department of University of Bonn, dedicated to exploring the analytical foundations of economics and finance. We bring together motivated students with a shared interest in data-driven methods, economic theory, and financial modeling. Through workshops, research projects, and guest talks, we create a collaborative environment to deepen our understanding of quantitative tools and their real-world applications. Whether you're looking to strengthen your academic skills or engage in applied research, QFE is a space to learn, connect, and grow.

Engagement

What We Do

At QFE Bonn, we work across three focused teams — Econ, Finance, and Quant — each dedicated to a specific area of interest:

  • Econ explores economic theory, policy analysis, and empirical research. We dive into current economic challenges using modern tools like econometrics, structural modeling, and causal inference.
  • Finance focuses on asset pricing, corporate finance, and financial markets. Our members study real-world market behavior, build valuation models, and follow current developments in global finance.
  • Quant is where data science meets economics and finance. This team works with coding, machine learning, and statistical modeling to solve complex analytical problems.

Together, we host regular events, collaborate on research projects, and support each other in academic and career development. Whether you're aiming to publish research, prepare for industry roles, or just learn something new, there's a place for you at QFE Bonn.
