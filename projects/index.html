What We Do in Our Sessions

Interactive workshops and guided hands-on projects to learn and improve your skills

Every QFE Bonn session features a step-by-step hands-on project that explores a key concept in econometrics, quantitative finance, AI/ML, or data analysis. We work through each part together, from cleaning data and testing models to building tools and drawing insights.

You don't need prior experience to join, every session comes with a prepared guide, clear goals, and support. You can explore our project log below to see what we've done so far.
